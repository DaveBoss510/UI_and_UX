No Ego Coffee

Humble yourself with a taste explosion coffee experience at No Ego Coffee. We source our single origin beans from Sumatra for the highest quality coffee beans in the world for our coffee enthused customers. We donate 20% of all our profits to helping stray dogs on Bali with the building of the first dog sanctuary. This rescue, fostering, and sanctuary center employs locals and educates locals to the humane treatment of dogs and all animals, while offering medical help and refuge to all the homeless, starving, and diseased dogs on Bali. We push to create positive change with modesty, compassion, and stoke.



Sourcing Sumatran Beans

We have stood on our Sumatran farmer's coffee field and hand picked the coffee fruits we wanted dried. We only use naturally dried, unwashed coffee beans.

Our Roasting Process

We batch roast our own beans at a commercial roastery on-Island in Kuta. We roast to varying darknesses but our light and medium roasts are our best sellers.

Our Locations

We have locations in Uluwatu, Canggu, and Kudungu. All in Bali. All on the west side of the island.
